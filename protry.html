Our Story

Navio started as a solution for everyday transportation problems which we used to face daily.

  • Higher taxi fares
  • Not so good public transport system in various cities
  • Maintenance and running cost of personal vehicles increasing day by day
  • Expensive EVs and rising price of petrol

We thought of doing something in this space and came up with the plan of using Hydrogen as our fuel choice. It is clean and sustainable but the current problem with hydrogen vehicles is that they are really expensive. The tech is new and few people are switching to H2 which is limiting the advancement of HFCEV's(Hydrogen Fuel cell Electric Vehicle). We knew from the start that if we want to sustain this technology and company we need to come up with end - to - end solution and that is when this project became a startup. Currently we are in the early stages of prototype development, with future looking bright as always we aim to become India's first commercially launched Hydrogen Mobility startup.

Our Product Ranges

Our end-to-end approach includes two product ranges designed specifically to solve the core problem of transportation.
– Power-train
– Fueling stations

Hydrogen Fuel Cell Power-train

Our design is unique and different from the competition since it is designed as a Modular block-based fuel cell. The advantage of this design is that it can be incorporated with any vehicle class with an increase/decrease of blocks to increase/decrease power output.

Mobile Refueller

Our USP product is the mobile fueling station which can be installed anywhere there is a ground and water supply, be it the garage of your house or the remotest location. You can top-up your vehicles in a jiffy.
